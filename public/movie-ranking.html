Sandro's Top 6 Favorite Movies

My top 6 favorite movies of all time.

Top Gun (1986)

Not because I was born in 1986. Growing up, I actually didn't like Top Gun at all. Later on in life, I've grown to love it.

Cobra (1986)

Again, not due to my birth year. Easily one of the best classic Stallone films out there.

Terminator II (1991)

Mom and I watched this when I was little, fond memories. And yes, the ending STILL gets my teary-eyed.

Independence Day (1996)

Little did I know, at the time, this movie would be the gateway for my love of fighter jets.

The Good, The Bad, and The Ugly (1966)

Grandma's favorite. Whatever happened to Western classics?

Only The Strong (1993)

The first film I'd seen with Mark Dacascos. In this, he gathers a group of teenagers and mentors them on the art of Capoeira.
